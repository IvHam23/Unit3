Buying and Selling Online


StockX

4.5* In App Store (Resell)


StockX a variety of options to choose from. They have Sneakers, Clothes, Collectibles, Handbags, and Watches to choose from. For the most part they are very reliable and the top streetwear marketplace now with only a few incidents of fake apparel and/or minor damages. Note that people only buy/sell NEW items on this site. This is a good site to see what the market value of streetwear items are to compare.


Goat

4.8* In App Store (Resell)


Goat's marketplace mainly focuses on sneakers. For the past couple of months, they have been selling some designer clothes. Depending on the supply, they do sell used sneakers at a fraction of the price. They are very trustworthy and professional. Ordering from them is very easy. There are not a lot of hassles and there is a fixed shipping price no matter the item.


Grailed

4.8* In App Store (Resell)


Grailed's marketplace is completely from sellers using the app. There is a scaled on how used an items listed is from "New/Never Worn" to "Very Worn" as well as not specified. There are also many filters like size, seller location, and price that it would be easy to pin point the exact item you are trying to find within your price range. Grailed does not have an authentication checker on items. Instead, they do checks from reviews and comment on different sellers to show buyers with a tag if they are trusted or untrusted. Usually the untrusted users are removed. They focus on the main streetwear brands as well as some designer brands.

Retailers

Retailers like adidas, nike, footlocker, champs, and more sell streetwear for the msrp price. For popular releases, there are usually bought from these retailers and resold on sites like StockX. But theses sites are where people can buy streetwear at the minimum price.
