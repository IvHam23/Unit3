Buying and Selling in Stores (Toronto Area)



Essentials


Essential is a small boutique that sells the main brands of streetwear. Even though it is a small store, it is filled to the brim with streetwear items. They mainly sell the popular streetwear brands. One thing that makes them stand out from other stores is that they have some old "grails" that are hard to get (especially new) that they have on display and for sale. The funny thing is that they are in the same indoor mall as Kenshi
