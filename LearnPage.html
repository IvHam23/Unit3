FAQs and General Knowledge



Streetwear is a style of casual clothing which became global in the 1990s. It grew from California surfskate culture and New York hip hop fashion to encompass elements of sportswear, punk and Japanese street fashion. Eventually haute couture became an influence. It commonly centers on "casual, comfortable pieces such as jeans, T-shirts, baseball caps, and sneakers", and exclusivity through intentional product scarcity. Enthusiasts follow particular brands and try to obtain limited edition releases.

The top 5 most popular brands right now are:

- Supreme, they are the most popular in the industry and most of their main apparel and accessories usually sell out depending on the "hype".


- Bape, great cool idea with different colour camos with the apes but quite expensive and harder to get (does not usually go up in price).


- Off-White, most of their apparel is more expensive because it is a designer brand.


- Anti Social Social Club, their logo/words is what they are known for and they always sell out when they release something within a couple of days at most.


- Stussy, interessting and cool apparel. Their items are leaning more towards art than apparel. Reasonable price and does not usually sell out.

- "Deadstock" = Brand new, unworn

- "Never Seen the Floor" = (usually shoes) Meaning the item has not been taken out of box/pakaging (also reffers to Deadstock)

- "Backdoor" = This is when employees at retail stores take popular items and save it for themselves to purchase

Retail is the price someone would pay if they went to the manufacturer's store or venders that supply it. It is usually called "MSRP". Resell is when someone buys an item and sells it to another person at a differnt price. View buy/sell page for more info on resellers. Most use websites like StockX to sell their items and StockX would take a portion of it as the middle man.

The top 5 coolest and most interessting collaborations:

- Supreme x Oreo (2020): It came in a pack of 3 for $3 USD. It is a red oreo cookie that had a small supreme wording indented on both sides of the cookie

- Surpeme x Honda (2019): Supreme partnered with Honda to make some apparel and an acutal working motorbike that people could buy.

- Bape x Marvel, Star Wars, Mikey Mouse, Dragon Ball z, and Various Artists: Bape partnered with these enterprises and people listed to create apparel that have their iconic ape with the person and or thing beside the it.

- Nike Air Jordans x Off White (Virgil Abloh): Artist and owner of Off-White Virgil Abloh partnered with Nike Air Jordan to create some special nike shoes with Abloh's touch. It is a beautiful collection with many more paris coming out later this year. Note that these shows resell value keeps on going up and is a high price to pay
