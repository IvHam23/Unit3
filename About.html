About "The Streetwear Archives"


About the Website

This website was created to teach the user general knowledge about the ever growing community of streetwear. Since it attracts mostly older teens to young adults, older adults will have a harder time understanding what they are getting for their younger peoples. Some items can be very expensive and since these streetwear brands are not designer, aduts will stray away from these items because they are unfamiliar with it. This website will help knowledge anyone and it will give them some trustworthy stores and websites that sell authentic items if they wish to browse or shop.
