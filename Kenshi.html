Buying and Selling in Stores (Toronto Area)



Kenshi


Kenshi is a small boutique that sells the main brands of streetwear. Even though they are a small store, they have a good amount of items in their inventory. It is inside a indoor mall in downtown Toronto so it might be confusing to find the store. Their prices are relatively competitive and they only sell authentic and mostly new items.
